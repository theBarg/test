It is not in fact clear what approach Kinbote is taking to his writing. Notes like this suggest that he is working through the poem methodically, writing out each note sequentially and never going back over his own work. However, his frequent referencing of notes that occur sometimes hundreds of lines after the one he is currently writing suggest that the commentary is, in fact, planned out and edited. This inconsistency could be a sign that Kinbote is not writing the commentary at all (see theories on authorship.)
